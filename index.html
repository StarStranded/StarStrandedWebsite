Cooperative Survival in the Endless Void.

In the cold, unforgiving expanse of the cosmos, a shared destiny awaits. Star Stranded isn't just a game; it's a desperate race against time and the unknown. You and 1-3 fellow crew members are marooned, with only one hope: constructing a vessel capable of escaping to distant, stable star systems. Every decision matters as you meticulously gather crucial ship resources – all while carefully charting your course to avoid star systems that promise certain doom. This isn't about outwitting your friends; it's about surviving with them. Your combined intellect, strategic foresight, and unwavering trust are your only currency in the void.

The Perilous Ascent: Oxygen, Stability, and Destiny

Your survival in Star Stranded hinges on more than just parts; it's about the very air you breathe and the ground beneath your feet. As you construct your escape vessel, each Floor and Wall you place is a delicate act of engineering. But be warned: All construction risks a catastrophic collapse that will threaten your precious oxygen reserves and ships trajectory. Run out of O2, and your mission is over. This isn't just building; it's a high-stakes, breathless dance of precision and foresight. Will you reach the necessary heights before the air runs out? With 42 possible Floors to build, each one propels your vessel further into the cosmos, correlating directly to its power and the distance it can travel. But the journey is fraught with peril. Critical resources like engines, navigation systems, and the cockpit can only be supported on specific, limited Floors, demanding careful planning. What's more, each Floor presents a unique objective – often a precise roll of the dice – that can determine the fate of the crew. Every new level is a gamble, a test of skill, and a step closer to a new home. Can you build a ship powerful enough to reach safety?

A New Galaxy, Every Time.

No two games of Star Stranded will ever be the same. Every playthrough offers a fresh challenge and a new narrative. The dynamic nature of resource acquisition and the unpredictable perils of space ensure that your strategies will constantly evolve. Discover new pathways to victory, uncover hidden dangers, and adapt to the ever-shifting landscape of the galaxy. Star Stranded isn't just a game for one night; it's a gateway to countless epic journeys and endless hours of cooperative fun.
